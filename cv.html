Story

In June 2024, I traveled to Busan, South Korea.
It was my first time traveling alone, and also the first time
I truly stepped into the sea.

A Day at Busan
That day, as the only foreigner, I learned surfing with a bunch of locals.

I never expected that someone who couldn't even swim would be able to stand on a surfboard and actually ride
a wave for a while.

That moment gave me infinite courage. I've maintained that fearless drive until now...
We're afraid of many things, but sometimes all it takes is trying once. What we gain can be far more than just the experience of trying.
